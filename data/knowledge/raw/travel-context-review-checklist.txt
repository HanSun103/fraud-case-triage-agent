Travel context review checklist for the demo:

If travel notice is on file, analysts should verify whether the location change aligns with the declared travel window before treating location mismatch as a strong signal.

Known device plus travel notice usually reduces concern unless there is also unusual amount growth, rapid repeat attempts, or a merchant pattern that conflicts with recent customer behavior.

New device plus foreign merchant plus elevated amount should still receive closer review even when travel is plausible.

Analyst notes should state whether travel context was verified, assumed, missing, or contradicted by other evidence.
